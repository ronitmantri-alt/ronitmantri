Welcome

Hey, I’m Ronit. This website is my personal corner of the internet — a place where I share what I build, what I experiment with, and how I keep leveling up my skills. I’m deeply interested in technology, creativity, and turning ideas into things that actually work.

I don’t believe in boring projects or empty designs. I like things that feel alive, interactive, and purposeful. Whether it’s a website, a game, or a tool, I want it to feel clean, smooth, and fun to use. This site is a reflection of that mindset.

Everything here is about growth — learning faster, building smarter, and pushing ideas further than before.

About Me

I’m someone who doesn’t just use technology — I take it apart, understand it, and rebuild it better. I enjoy creating things from scratch, especially using HTML and web technologies. Clean structure, real functionality, and thoughtful design matter a lot to me.

I strongly believe in real freedom when it comes to tools and software. If something claims to be free but hides paywalls, limits, or ads everywhere, I’m not interested. I like projects that are open, usable, and honest — and that philosophy carries into everything I create.

I have a builder’s mindset. One moment I’m designing detailed LEGO structures, carefully planning stability and form. The next moment I’m experimenting in a makeshift chemistry lab, creating reactions and making volcanoes erupt just to see how things behave. It’s controlled chaos — experimenting, testing, learning, and improving.

That mix of structure and creativity defines how I think. LEGO teaches patience and precision. Chemistry teaches cause and effect. Tech combines both. I don’t like shallow understanding — I want to know why things work, not just that they work.

Outside of tech, I love adventure and nature. I enjoy hiking, biking, snorkeling, and exploring new places. I’ve hiked Mount Bromo, seen active volcanoes, and snorkeled deep waters in places like Hawaii and Borneo. Exploration — both physical and digital — is a huge part of who I am.

  • Builder: I design and create projects from the ground up
  • Experimenter: I test ideas and learn through action
  • Explorer: I enjoy discovery, challenge, and growth
